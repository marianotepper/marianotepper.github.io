Color Projection Exhibit Nasher Museum of Art

The project, now part of the museum’s permanent exhibit, allows users to digitally recolor medieval sculpture using mobile and projection technologies. The idea is to provide a fun and interactive platform to visually demonstrate how four medieval statues might have appeared in their originality.

Autism & Beyond

Autism & Beyond is a study of young children's mental health that seeks to better understand and identify risks for development. The study aims to test new video technology that can analyze a child’s emotion and behavior.

NMF for big data

Nonnegative matrix factorization is undergoing challenges in recent years mainly because of the increasingly growing size of the datasets. In this work, we introduce NMF algorithms for such big data problems.
