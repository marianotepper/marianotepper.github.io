Autism & Beyond

We are a team of child psychiatrists, psychologists, pediatricians, engineers and computer scientists at Duke University. We work together to create new ways to support parents and young children's healthy development.

Autism & Beyond is a groundbreaking new study of childhood mental health powered by Apple’s ResearchKit. The study aims to test new video technology that can analyze a child’s emotion and behavior.

We hope that this technology may one day be used to screen young children in their homes for autism and mental health challenges, such as anxiety or tantrums. We want parents to have tools that will help them understand their children and find help if they need it.
